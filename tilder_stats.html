Notes on Tilder's Stats

  1. Halfling:
    • Opponents surprise at -4 to hit when halflings are alone and not in metal armor, or when in a group of halflings and/or elves, or when opponents are 90' away.
    • Attribute modifiers -1 STR, +1 DEX taken into account.
  2. STR:
    • open doors 6; bend bars 2%
  3. CON:
    • system shock 90%; resurrect survival 94%
  4. INT:
    • native languages: halfling, (alignment)
    • 4 additional languages: common, elvish, orcish, dwarvish
  5. HP:
    • d6 + 1 (CON) per level
  6. AC:
    • Leather armor [AC 8] taken into account.
    • -4 AC DEX reaction bonus also taken into account, but when surprised or unable to react, this bonus is negated.
    • Additional -4 AC defense bonus can be applied when chosing to defend instead of attacking.
  7. THAC0:
    • +3 (DEX) and +1 (Halfling) to hit with missile attacks.
    • -3 to hit with non-proficient weapon.
    • +2 to hit (Tumbling) when unarmed.
  8. Weapon:
    • Initially 2 weapons of proficiency; 1 more every 4 levels.
    • Weapons of proficiency denoted by (p) in Inventory.
  9. Saves:
    • +1 (Halfling) for every 3.5 CON points on Poison, Rod/Staff/Wand and Spell saving throws (taken into account).
  10. Thieving Skills:
    • 30% per level to be split among all 8 skills, with no more than 15% going to any one skill.
    • Open Locks and Remove/Find Traps skills may only be applied once.
  11. Tumbling A successful tumble means:
    • If fall less than 60', then take half damage.
    • If fall less than 10', then no damage.
    • If attacking unarmed, then +2 to hit.
