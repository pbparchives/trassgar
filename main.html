How to Play
We are now writing and playing parts of this game on a Yahoo! club dedicated to this world. It is a private club--by invitation only. If you would like to join the game, read the stories as posted here and send an e-mail expressing your interest. Send the e-mail to the_almightydm@yahoo.com.

New and current players read below for guidelines on writing the adventures.

    If you are joining this adventure, here's what you need to do:
  1. Roll up a 1st level player character from either 1st or 2nd Edition Advanced Dungeons & Dragons. Mostly I use 2nd Edition rules, but I am apt to break out the original manuals every now and then for referecne. Look at Gnash's character sheet for an example of what kind of character information we're using. (For instance, we are playing with alignment, but since that is not generally shared, it does not appear on the character sheet.)
  2. Write a background/introduction text for your character, which should include how he or she came to be in Trassgar. As the original players know, there are magic portals in several cities in Trassgar which are actually portals to and from other worlds. Your character could come from Toril or Krynn or an established AD&D world, and find himself in Trassgar by way of these portals.
  3. E-mail me your character sheet and background/introduction. I'll find a good plot to insert your character into and we can get rolling!
  4. The style of play here is narrative-based. That is, I, the DM, write a scenario which you, the player, will react to by writing a counter-scenario. The prose should be third person, so that it reads like a novel would read. Rule intrusions are placed in [differently colored text between square brackets, like this]. In the interesting of playing faster than a snail's pace, the player characters are at liberty to make some die rolls that the DM normally would make. For instance, you can write both sides of combat. I trust you to make fair die rolls. Besides, if you're playing then it's for fun. Why fudge them? Plot lines written at the Yahoo! club will be transferred here periodically.
  5. The world of Trassgar, its history, cities, native monsters, and all other related items are the brainchildren of Stacy Assaf. You are still encouraged to use creative license, but all alterations or additions to aspects of Trassgar are subject to review by the Trassgarian Planetary Society. (i.e. Stacy Assaf)
