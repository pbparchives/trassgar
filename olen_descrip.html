Olen Orpheas

 tall, definitly elven but predominatly human looking. medium, human-like build, not elven slender build. elven qualities- somewhat pointy ears, slender eyes, cheek bones-- majority of elven features come out in the face. wears a standard clothes of a Wen Kingdom semi-noble magic-user, his native homeland before transport to Trassgar. Several sacks and pouches hold rudimentary spell components, minimal survival gear, currency. Over normal garb is a tough thick white robe made of Ice City-native snow monster pelt. Finely tailored for a hood and less ragged edges. Coat has two crude pockets on each side hold other objects. In one hand is a light walking staff, almost his full height of six feet and an inch. Oddly enough, the 'quirky wizard' has gold-speckled eyes, the sign of his protection under the mystical dragon he met in a recent adventure.

personality is cordial, but often sarcastic. Quick sense of humor, and an analytical mind. Often trusts in spells he knows and has practice with, such as chromatic orb and web.
