Trassgar: Ice City
== a part of The Trassgar Saga ==
Webdate: November 24, 2009

This city is the home of the famed hermit/healer Selanass. On the road to this city from Central Trassgar, there are dangerous avalanches, ice monsters, fanged pterovultures, narrow roads and deep chasms. Only the bravest of travelers dare make the journey to the intentionally secluded fortress of Ice City.

During the Original Adventures, the Trassgar adventurers traveled to Ice City to pick up Gnash from a long recuperation at the hands of its healers. Along the way they discovered the Ancient Prophecy of Bolario:

From the Cave of Punishment
To the Altar of Ashes you brought me.
You climbed to the Tree of Struggling
And formed me.
And now you must place me on my
Courtyard of Ashes.

This prophesy of Bolario was fulfilled by the adventurers, and the ancient library of Ice City was finally revealed after being sealed for centuries. The healers Gallimand and Sellyano were quite happy of the opening of the library, and Gnash was very happy to see some secular faces and leave the isolated fortress!
