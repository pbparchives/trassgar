The Trassgar Saga's Original Adventures
== a page of The Trassgar Saga ==
Webdate: 2000.10.02

Prologue: The Journey ??.??.1996
The original adventure, pre-Trassgar. First apperance of eventual party regulars Olen Orpheas (as Boxer Boy) and Slahm. Players: Boxer Boy, Slahm, Spice, Thalen, Zara.
The New World Aug.19.1996
The journey to Trassgar via the magic portal and rescue of a missing friend, Stor. Don't forget the pink mushrooms! First appearance of Amber, Gargamel, Slick, and eventually, Stor Restyl. Players: Amber, Boxer Boy, Gargamel, Slahm, Slick, Stor.
The Road to Ice City Aug.22.1996
The perilous journey to Ice City to retrieve a recuperating warrior. First appearance of Gnashuphagnewtognius Gnarly. Recall the ancient Ice City Prophesy. Players: Amber, Boxer Boy, Gargamel, Stor. Voted the favorite original adventure by unanimous vote of the original players!
Fire in Forest's Edge Aug.31.1996
A trio of fiesty females joins the group, and headquarters is set up in Forest's Edge at Klive's High Safe Inn. Traveling to Himm to follow a lead. First apperance of Beer-sheeba, Nebuchadrezzar and Scurge. Players: Amber, Beer-sheeba, Gargamel, Gnash, Nebuchadrezzar, Olen Orpheas [nee Boxer Boy], Slahm, Scurge, Stor.
What's wrong with Himm? Sept.28.1996
The mystery of the silence of Himm is uncovered, as well as a nasty creature. A chaggrin grue elemental attacks and kills Marianna. First (and last) appearance of Marianna, Klive's niece. Players: Amber, Gargamel, Gnash, Marianna, Olen Orpheas, Stor.
Himm Mountain Dec.30.1996
The rescue of the Himm miners and the release of an ancient golden dragon (Eebnox). First appearance of Mike. Players: Amber, Gargamel, Gnash, Mike, Olen, Stor.
Scrub Shack Jan.20.1997
Commissioned on a quest by the enigmatic Scrub Shack, but not before Slahm buys a falcon. Test of the scorpions. Players: Amber, Gargamel, Gnash, Mike, Olen, Slahm, Stor.
Journey to Furterville... Feb.16?.1997
...and the siege of the Castle of the Third House of Trassgar. Players: Amber, Gargamel, Gnash, Olen, Scurge, Slahm, Stor.
