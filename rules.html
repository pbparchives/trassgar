Determining Initiative
To determine initiative for a combat round, roll d10 per side in the conflict. Lowest roll wins initiative. If more than one side rolls the same initiative roll, action for those sides happens simultaneously.

To hit or not to hit
To determine if you successfully attack someone in combat, subtract your opponent's armor class (AC) from your THAC0. This is the number you have to get or beat on a roll of a d20.
